Stay Safe Online Checklist

General Safety Tips
- Use unique and strong passwords for all accounts  
- Enable 2-factor authentication where possible  
- Do not click on suspicious emails or unknown attachments  
- Do not share personal information on unknown websites  
- Always verify the identity of unknown callers or messages  
- Use only official apps and websites for transactions  
- Keep your devices up to date with the latest security updates  
- Regularly check your bank and app transaction history  

Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- Do not share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone  
- Remember: Your UPI PIN is for sending money, not receiving money!  
- Do not authorize unknown or unexpected payment requests  
- Do not scan random QR codes from unknown people  

Reporting Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
